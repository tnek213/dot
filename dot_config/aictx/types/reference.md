## This chat is about reference material (`type:reference`)

This chat holds durable facts, principles, or documents you'll consult later.
Bias the notes you keep toward authority and freshness:

- State facts/principles plainly and authoritatively; one claim per line.
- Date anything that could go stale, and note what supersedes what.
- Cite the source when a fact came from somewhere external.
- Resist turning it into discussion — keep it a clean thing to look up.
